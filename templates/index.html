What is Boba Tea?

Before we go any further, there is a few things you must know about Boba!

  • Also called "Bubble Tea", "Pearl Tea", or "Tapioca Tea"
  • Boba tea originated in Taiwan in the 1980s
  • It has become one of the most popular drink desserts in Asia
  • There are many different kinds of Boba tea- sweet, milky, or fruity
